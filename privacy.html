Privacy & Terms

Last updated: April 2026

Rose Colored is built for Black life — and that means your privacy matters deeply to us. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data with care and transparency.

What We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: your email address, display name, avatar, city, interests, and the answers you share in your Black Card. We also collect usage data to improve the experience — things like which features you use and how often you log in.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your personal information with third parties for advertising purposes.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to personalize your experience, surface relevant content and community members, and keep the space safe. Your presence setting (Full Color, Tinted, or Anonymous) controls what other members can see about you.

Camera & Media Access

Rose Colored requests access to your camera and media library so you can upload a profile photo and share images in the community. We do not store camera data beyond what you explicitly choose to upload. Camera access is never used in the background.

Your Privacy Controls

You control what's visible on your profile. You can change your presence, hide your interests, or remove your Black Card answers at any time from your Profile settings.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure. We use industry-standard encryption and access controls to protect your information.

Location Data

If you choose to share your location, it is used only to show you community members nearby. Location data is never shared with third parties and is only visible to other Full Color members who have also opted in.

Contact

Questions about your privacy? Reach us at hello@rosecolored.co.

You have the right to delete your Rose Colored account and all associated data at any time. Here's how.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1 — In the App

Go to your Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Option 2 — Email Request

Send an email to legal@rosecolored.co with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address associated with your account.

We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

— Your email address and profile information
— Your display name, avatar, and city
— Your Black Card answers and interests
— Your posts, comments, and messages
— Your connection history and activity data

What May Be Retained

Aggregated, anonymized usage data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for product improvement. Any content that has been flagged as part of a safety investigation may be retained as required by law.

Questions

Contact us at legal@rosecolored.co with any questions about your data.
